Notes App

     This native Android Java app loads notes from google's firebase databse and reads them with a text to speech API. Notes can easily be sourced form quizlet and inserted into the database with just a few clicks.

Augmented Reality

     Integrating the vuforia API in Unity 3D this mobile application allows students to view a virtual microscope on their desk. A lecturer could send questions during a lecture and the listiner can answer by selecting or solving parts of a an interactive 3D puzzel.

Portal

Atomic simulation
