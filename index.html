Language Learning App

     This android native language learning app called "Be Fluient" was created with Java and android studio. It syncs lessons and progress for all users in a central databse. This allows users to be alerted when their score has been surpassed by friends so they can play more and catch up. The app uses text to speech as well as voice recognition in multiple languages. It uses the speech recognition to awards points based on how well the phrases are pronounced. The words are spaced out and translated and tested individually and in groups and also revisited at an opportune time to maximize retention.
